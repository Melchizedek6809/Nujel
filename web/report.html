Nujel performance report

These measurement's are pretty bad for comparing Nujel with other languages, since these benchmarks are in no way representative of a normal workload. They mainly serve to make sure that Nujel performance doesn't regress too badly during development. Another important aspect is to infer how hard a certain performance goal is to achieve, and how far away from a performance ceiling Nujel is for certain micro benchmarks.
